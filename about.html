The Origins

Engineering Floating Silhouettes

Founded in Paris, ANTIGRAVITY was established to explore the physics of motion within haute couture. Our founder envisioned a house where fabrics could float, garments could breathe, and modern tech-infused tailoring could coexist with heritage draping.

We reject the constricting corsetry of the past. Instead, we study how light silk organza, structural wool crepe, and technical carbon-composites interact with gravity to form moving art.

"Fashion should not anchor us; it should allow us to glide weightlessly above the everyday."
Aesthetic Principles

Futuristic Minimalism meets Heritage Craft

We design limited-run drops, releasing exactly 15 meticulously configured styles each season. By eschewing mass production, we commit to absolute precision. Each style is individually serialized, making every piece an exclusive artifact of craftsmanship.

Material Engineering Laboratory
Craft & Conscience

100% Ethical Sourcing

Luxury is meaningless without responsibility. ANTIGRAVITY works exclusively with historic family-owned mills in Lyon and Biella that employ closed-loop water systems and solar power.

Our metallic silver brocades and gold filaments are sourced from recycled metals, and our silk crepe-de-chine is organic and cruelty-free. Every artisan in our workshop is supported by living wage standards and works in a spaces that fosters creative health.
