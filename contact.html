Atelier Policies & Care

All ANTIGRAVITY products are shipped globally via secure insured courier service. For international purchases, custom duties and VAT are calculated during order checkouts to ensure seamless delivery to your doorstep.
Since our seasonal pieces are produced in limited edition drops, we accept returns on unworn ready-to-wear items within 14 days of receipt. Custom bespoke and altered pieces are tailored uniquely to you and cannot be returned.
Handcrafting a custom bespoke garment requires between 6 to 10 weeks depending on complexity, fabric selection, and fitting calendar availability. Rush commissions may be accommodated under our VIP concierge service tier.
Our organic silks, heritage wools, and metallic thread details are delicate. We strongly recommend professional museum-grade dry cleaning. Store garments on padded luxury hangers away from direct sunlight.
