Bespoke Process

The Journey of Creation

A bespoke commission is a dialogue between client, silhouette, and fabric. We host physical consultations in our Paris showroom or virtual consultations globally.

Step 01

Initial Consultation

Discuss silhouettes, style profiles, lifestyle needs, and visual aesthetics in person or via video link.

Step 02

Conceptual Draping & Sketches

We present two customized render concepts alongside physical swatches of proposed materials.

Step 03

Atelier Fitting Session

A canvas fitting toile is constructed to configure the pattern uniquely to your proportions.

Step 04

Handcrafting the Silhouette

Artisans build the final piece over 80–120 labor hours with couture finishes.
